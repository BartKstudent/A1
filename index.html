Project Ideas

Overview

This will be an automation system that allows gyms and personal training studies sign up enquiries via Internet or over the phone and send them on a digital onboarding journey without delay caused by manual processing. Signed up members will have immediate access to exclusive membership area filled with educational material, workouts, and general health guidance. Their data will automatically be added to existing CRM used by business and subscribed to regular direct debit payments outlined during sign up process. Member’s will be automatically subscribed to gym’s newsletter mailing list to stay up to date with any changes and events at their training facility.

Motivation

According to (Heather Sarantis, 2002) statistically we have an upward trend of paper use in businesses worldwide. That creates a situation, where businesses are less sustainable, less efficient, and less profitable, as the time goes by. The main goal of this project is to generate a positive effect for the business, the client, and the environment by allowing the company to move to paperless solutions that are more effective, more user friendly, much easier to access and less time-consuming. By creating efficient workflow automation, we can achieve all of these goals, for a much lower price in a timely manner.

Description

  1. Enquiry management – any enquiries coming through the Facebook ads, etc. will be automatically added to CRM database using existing Zapier integrations or custom Webhooks (if API is available for the CRM platform). Enquiry details will also be forwarded to relevant sales staff for follow-up. The sales team will then call the lead and use the phone sales forms (point 2 and 3) to go through the sales process.

  2. Phone Sales (Admin Side) – when committing to a phone sale the sales representative will use an online form containing the sales script and form fields where the team member can submit answers from the prospect. This way the staff can easily follow the script and build rapport while at the same time collecting information about the new sign up. Upon submission of the form the data will get updated in the CRM or sent to relevant staff for processing.

  3. Phone Sales (Client Side) – as part of the phone sales process the prospect will get a 3-stage form to collect their Terms and Conditions acknowledgement, personal data, payment details and point of sale referrals.

    • Terms and Conditions step – this step will ask for first name, last name email and contain a simple overview of terms and conditions followed by link to full Ts&Cs, acknowledgement tick box and submission button. Submission of the form will create a PDF containing the full terms and conditions and that the acknowledgement has been confirmed electronically by “First Name” “Last Name” with “Email” from “IP Address”. That Paperwork will be automatically emailed to both parties and to the CRM. Client will then be taken to the next form.

    • Client Details step – this step will be used to collect more personal data, such as mobile number, address, any existing health conditions, and payment details. Upon submission the data will be transferred to CRM, new membership will be created, and automatic direct debit plan set up. Both parties will get email confirmation of the submission, as well as automatically generated PDF outlining the provided information and membership package chosen. New login credentials will be created for the membership site and automated text with this information will be sent to the member.

    • Point of Sale Referral step – in this step new client will have the option to refer up to 3 people they know for a free 1-week trial at the facility. If any data is provided in this step sales staff will be sent an automated email with referral details. Whether data is provided or not, client will be taken to a “Thank You” page with outline of next steps.

  4. Online Sales – this will be available for anyone who is interested in signing up directly via website. This will be very similar to the Phone Sales (Client Side), but with a bit more information to guide them through the process. This can also be used on a tablet for a face to face sign-up in the facility.

  5. Membership Area – created with a simple WordPress plugin will allow anyone with login details to access restricted, exclusive content including onboarding, workouts, nutritional information, recipes, cooking guides, exercise library and more. It will be integrated with other software using APIs to minimize the time required for admin work.
